Learning in the round at Wintringham Primary Academy

[1]

Wintringham Primary Academy is an evolution of our concept for Four Dwellings Primary.

Both schemes place importance on learning in the landscape, with classrooms that connect to the outside. However, instead of the school hall, a tree-filled grove is placed in the centre. Flexibility is also inherent in the design – we designed ‘soft spots’ within the engineered timber structure that allow for spatial changes to adapt to the school’s future growth and evolving curriculum.
